Site Name

Smart Recipe Portal - This name reflects the purpose of the site: a dynamic and intelligent recipe portal that helps users easily discover, save, and share recipes. The name is simple, descriptive, and relevant to its content.

Site Purpose

The site aims to provide a rich, interactive space for users to explore categorized recipes (e.g., fitness, desserts, quick meals), filter them dynamically, submit their own, and save favorites using localStorage.

User Scenarios

  • What is a quick and healthy dinner I can make in under 30 minutes?
  • Can I submit my grandma’s cookie recipe to the platform?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #8e3b46 (used in titles, navigation, accents)
Secondary Color: #f1dca7 (used for backgrounds and call-to-action areas)

Typography

Heading Font: "Playfair Display", serif – for an elegant, recipe-magazine style
Body Font: "Open Sans", sans-serif – for readability and a modern look
